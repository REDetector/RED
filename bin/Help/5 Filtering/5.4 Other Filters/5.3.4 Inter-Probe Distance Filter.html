Inter-Probe Distance Filter

This filter allows you to select probes based on the distance to another probe. You could use it to identify clusters of probes, or isolated probes. You could also use it to select groups of probes with a specific periodicity.

Distance measurements are taken from the end of one probe to the start of the other.

Options

  1. The minimum and maximum distance between probes (only one needs to be specified)
  2. Whether the distance spanned can pass over another probe
