5 Reports

The Reports section allows you to construct a report based on the filtration and statistical tests illustrated before. The reports are based on probeset/probelists or data from database. There are three ways to present RNA-editing sites, which are Variant Distribution, Sites Distribution and Filter Reports. The report plot can be exported as described in Saving Images section. If you need more RNA-editing sites information, you can export the result in a text format.
